About this map

A personal project by Lars Heimann, visualizing the running routes I recorded with a Garmin watch while living in Münster from 2021 to 2025.

The brighter a street gets, the more often I ran it. Starts and finishes are trimmed before publishing, so the map focuses on routes rather than doorsteps.
